About Us

If your financial books are untidy or you need advice on how to properly manage your funds, turn to the highly experienced accountants at Kays Lundgren St Germain & Co. in Thief River Falls, MN. We love helping clients resolve their financial concerns.

We'll assess what's going on so that we can provide you with relevant information and appropriate guidance to suit your needs and address your concerns. Our goal is to answer your questions thoroughly so that you can make better choices going forward. We're dedicated to satisfying our clients, and we offer flexible appointment availability throughout the week to make it easier for you to come in and meet with us.

Fiebiger Swanson West & Co. was founded in 1972, and we've continued to offer a wide array of services to residents of the Fargo community for more than 40 years. Working with an accountant could help you get back on track. We want to make your life easier when it comes to personal or business accounting, so don't hesitate to contact us today to schedule a complete assessment.

Our Services

Accounting

Our friendly professionals will work diligently to perform the services you expect when hiring a local accountant. We'll do everything we can to streamline your home or office accounting practices.You can count on us for exceptional customer service no matter what the issue at hand. From account reconciliation to small business bookkeeping, we handle a wide range of concerns for a diverse assortment of clients.

Tax Preperation

If you're tired of struggling each year to compile and submit your annual return, rely on Kays Lundgren St Germain & Co. for comprehensive tax preparation. Whether you run a small business, perform work as a contractor, or hold down a traditional nine-to-five job, we've got the knowledge and skills to tackle your quarterly or annual returns. We want to be the firm you can depend on for thorough guidance on a wide range of tax situations.

Agricultural

If you work in agriculture, you probably have enough on your plate without having to worry about getting your finances in order. We understand the complexities involved in running and managing a farm or agricultural operation. When you work with us, you can rest assured that we'll customize our services to suit your needs. Whether you need help reconciling your cash accounts, keeping tabs on your inventory, or depreciating your equipment, our team is at your service and up to the challenge.

Bookkeeping

Whether you're a small business owner, a manager at a mid-sized corporation, or a self-employed freelancer, our team is at your service. We offer comprehensive bookkeeping services designed to keep your finances in check. Our bookkeeping services are designed to make your life easier so that you can focus on what you do best.

Auditing

No matter the size of your business, it's important to make sure you're keeping proper tabs on your finances while adhering to generally accepted accounting standards and laws. Our team of highly skilled accounting professionals can review your books and practices to make sure you're following the law and making the most of your revenue stream.

Small Business

There's no question that accounting is of key importance to the success of any business. Let Kays Lundgren St Germain & Co. manage your books, balance your ledgers, and go through all the number-crunching to save your business money and help you maintain your bottom line.
